About

I’m obsessed with technological revolutions and the role that software plays in empowering people at scale, shifting the culture en masse, and leveling the playing field for all.

My subjects of focus are design, media theory, and product & business strategy. Together, these have outsized impact on how technologies get created, distributed, and adopted to make life better for people and to liberate society’s creativity and self-expression.

I strongly believe we are on the precipice of a generation that will further catapult human progress unforeseen in the history of man. Best of all, we are really only at the beginning of this tipping point, with several emerging technologies advancing at the same time on top of previous advancements that were once nascent but have matured and stabilized overtime.

With billions of people having a supercomputer in their pocket, bandwidth becoming cheaper, and software changing virtually every industry, the opportunity is ripe and we must take advantage.

The future we want will not design itself.

Let’s get to work.
