Hi, I'm Jomi Cubol. I'm a product designer based in New York. My mission is to help build things that have a meaningful impact on society, creativity, and culture.

About

I love software, the internet, design, and technology. Every day I marvel at how these layers allow us to be smarter, more creative, and more connected. As as result, together we can become more aware, more expressive, more ourselves.

And I hope to add my contribution. To invent and help build products that are insightful, beautiful, and lasting. Things that empower more people through more access and more opportunity. Things that are cool. Things that make people feel good.

And I'm going to do whatever it takes to make that happen.

Recent Projects

Apploi Mobile 2.0 — Mobile interface and interaction design for Apploi, a job ecosystem seeking to reinvent the application process. The goal was to make it consistent with the newly designed and repurposed web consumer application, as well as increase engagement, repeat usage, and focus on Search as the core action and granular filtering for enhanced personalization. View project details.

Apploi Consumer Web 2.0 (Project Churchill) — Preliminary research, design sprints, system design, prototyping, and user testing to rethink Apploi's online job application process from the ground up. The objective was to make applying for a job as easy, effective, and enjoyable as possible to increase success rates and foster organic usage and growth. Most importantly, we wanted to radically improve the user's entire job searching and application experience. View project details. View finished product.
In collaboration with: C. Cassidy, G. Narich, T. Gebauer, M. Raton

Apploi Enterprise Dashboard 2.0 (Project Truman) — Information architecture, content strategy, and prototyping for Apploi's enterprise dashboard used by recruiters, hiring managers, and business owners. The goal was to improve the client side experience to make it easier to do individual actions (add jobs, see candidates, create templates) and team actions (permissions, administration, upgrades, etc.). View project details. View finished product.
In collaboration with: T. Gebauer, S. Hanson, G. Narich, M. Raton.
